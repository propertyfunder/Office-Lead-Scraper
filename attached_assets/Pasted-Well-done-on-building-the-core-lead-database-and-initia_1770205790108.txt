Well done on building the core lead database and initiating enrichment.

Now, let's improve the enrichment logic to increase the number of records with valid principal names and email addresses.

==================================================
🔁 SMARTER ENRICHMENT STRATEGY
==================================================

1. ✅ **Name & Email Fallbacks**
   If contact name or email is missing:
   - First, scan the company **homepage** for visible text patterns like:
     - `Owner`, `Founder`, `Director`, `About`, `Meet the Team`
     - Look for `<meta name="author">` and social media links too.
   - Instead of assuming `/about-us`, `/team`, etc., **crawl** and **parse homepage and top-level nav links** for names/emails.
   - Use `soup.find_all("a")` to dynamically extract team or contact pages instead of hardcoding URLs.

2. 🔎 **Companies House Lookup**
   - For any business with a valid company name and town, query **Companies House API**.
   - If found, extract **most recently appointed active director**.
   - Save in `contact_name`, and mark `enrichment_source = "companies_house"`.

3. 🔗 **LinkedIn Light Touch (No Scraping)**
   - Use search engine queries like:
     `"Site:linkedin.com/in/ {company name} {town}"`
   - If a match found, extract:
     - **Full Name**
     - **Job Title** (must be Owner, Director, Founder, Principal, etc.)
   - Save in `linkedin_url` and `contact_name`.

4. ✉️ **Email Strategy**
   - Accept Gmail or personal email addresses if:
     - The business is likely a sole trader.
     - It's the only email found.
   - Avoid using only `info@`, `contact@` unless no alternative exists.

5. 🗂️ **Tracking Enrichment Status**
   - Populate:
     - `enrichment_status`: `complete`, `missing_email`, `missing_name`, `incomplete`
     - `enrichment_source`: `website`, `linkedin`, `companies_house`, etc.

6. 🧼 **Avoid Duplicate Enrichment**
   - If a lead already has both `email` and `contact_name`, skip enrichment.
   - Only enrich leads missing one or both fields.
