Company website:
/about
/team
/clinicians
/staff
/leadership
/contact
/our-team
/meet-the-team
Companies House:
Pull active directors
Use director full names
LinkedIn (light search only, respect anti-scraping safeguards)
❌ Reject Contact Names That:
Are under 5 characters (unless clearly valid: e.g. Ali, Jo)
Contain numbers or symbols
Match business/structural words:
clinic, surgery, therapy, hospital, links, suite, road, centre, admin
Contain abnormal consonant clusters (e.g. Spjiok, Kpn, Gfi)
Fail vowel presence per word
Contain unusual trigram patterns
Use enhanced validation logic already implemented.
🏷 Add Column:
contact_name_validity = valid | suspicious | missing

Visible page text

footer/contact section

The best confirmed real email
Otherwise the best generic
